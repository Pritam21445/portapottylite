Frequently Asked Questions

What is your weather cancellation policy?

We offer a flexible weather policy. With 48 hours' notice, we can apply your full payment as a credit for a future rental. Rescheduling with less notice may incur a fee, as the unit has already been prepared and routed for delivery.

Can I schedule exact delivery and pickup times?

While we cannot guarantee a precise minute due to traffic, we do provide specific delivery windows (e.g., 8 AM - 12 PM) to ensure your unit arrives well before your event starts. Pickup times are flexible and can be arranged for the same evening or the following day.

What if a unit gets damaged during my event?

We offer an optional damage waiver for a small fee that covers most accidental damage. Please report any issues immediately. This waiver does not cover intentional vandalism, theft, or units that have been tipped over.

Are the chemicals you use safe?

Yes, absolutely. We only use high-quality, non-toxic, and biodegradable deodorizing solutions that are formaldehyde-free. They are completely safe for users and the environment, and all waste is disposed of according to strict environmental regulations.

Are your restrooms cleaned and sanitized?

Absolutely. Every single unit undergoes a rigorous, multi-point cleaning and sanitation process after each rental. We use hospital-grade disinfectants and deodorizers to ensure the unit you receive is sparkling clean and hygienic.
